Services

Our Procedures in Implantology :

Placement of Dental Implants Implant-Supported Prosthesis Immediate Dental Implants Pre-implant bone grafting Complete Reconstruction of the Arch

Placement of Dental Implants

Replacement of missing teeth with titanium implants, offering a durable, aesthetic and functional solution for restoring a harmonious smile.

Implant-Supported Prosthesis

Making and fitting crowns, bridges or full dentures on implants to restore dental function and aesthetics.

Immediate Dental Implants

Placement of implants immediately after tooth extraction, providing a rapid solution while minimising the time spent without a tooth.

Pre-implant bone grafting

Procedure aimed at restoring insufficient bone volume to allow for the placement of implants, ensuring a solid and stable foundation.

Complete Reconstruction of the Arch

Rehabilitation of the entire dental arch using multiple implants, allowing for the replacement of several missing teeth with fixed prosthetic solutions.

Our Procedures in Parodontologie :

Deep scaling and periodontal curettage Surgical Treatment of Periodontal Diseases Gingival Graft Labial and Lingual Frenectomy Guided Periodontal Regeneration Gingivectomy Periodontal Splinting Surgical Tooth Extraction Cyst Enucleation

Deep scaling and periodontal curettage

Subgingival cleaning to remove plaque and tartar, essential for halting the progression of periodontal diseases

Surgical Treatment of Periodontal Diseases

Surgical care aimed at preventing and treating infections of the gums and supporting structures of the teeth to prevent tooth loss.

Gingival Graft

Surgical interventions to cover gingival recessions and improve both the aesthetics and health of the gums.

Labial and Lingual Frenectomy

Correction of labial or lingual frena that cause aesthetic or functional problems, particularly in orthodontics or to facilitate dental implantation.

Guided Periodontal Regeneration

Surgical technique using membranes to promote the regeneration of bone and supporting tissues around teeth affected by periodontal diseases.

Gingivectomy

Surgical technique using membranes to promote the regeneration of bone and supporting tissues around teeth affected by periodontal diseases.

Periodontal Splinting

Periodontal splinting is a treatment aimed at stabilizing mobile teeth following loss of bony support caused by periodontitis. It strengthens the stability of the teeth by distributing chewing forces and helping to preserve dental alignment.

Surgical Tooth Extraction

Surgical extraction is performed when teeth are impacted or difficult to access, such as wisdom teeth. This procedure allows for the safe removal of teeth while minimizing risks and complications.

Cyst Enucleation

Cyst enucleation involves removing a cyst present in the bone or soft tissues. This surgery aims to eliminate infection and promote bone regeneration to restore oral health.

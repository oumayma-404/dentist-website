What is Periodontology ?

Periodontology is the dental specialty that focuses on the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the gums and supporting structures of the teeth. Its aim is to maintain the health of periodontal tissues. Periodontal diseases, such as gingivitis and periodontitis, can lead to tooth loss if left untreated. A periodontist is trained to manage these conditions, perform specific treatments and advise on best oral hygiene practices.

What is Implantology ?

Implantology is a branch of dentistry concerned with the placement of dental implants. Implants are titanium devices surgically inserted into the jawbone to replace the roots of missing teeth. They serve as a support for dental prostheses, such as crowns, bridges or dentures, restoring dental function and aesthetics. Implantology requires specialized training, as it involves a thorough understanding of dental anatomy and surgery.

What does a periodontist-implantologist do?

A periodontist-implantologist is a dentist who specializes in the treatment of periodontal disease and the placement of dental implants. They play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating gum problems, while paving the way for effective implant solutions. This specialist assesses the patient's overall oral health, performs surgical procedures to treat periodontal disease, and also performs dental implant placement when necessary. His expertise ensures successful treatment, guaranteeing better long-term oral health.
